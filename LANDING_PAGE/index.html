    Find your nearest hospital...

    Take a look at our exclusive list of hospitals that we have to offer.

    General and Medical Healthcare have an extensive list of private hospitals and medical facilities, which we call our 'Participating Hospitals'. The costs of treatment within different facilities can vary enormously so we reduce our premiums to reflect the lower costs of treatment at certain facilities.

    Our Participating Hospitals are grouped into 3 levels: ‘First Choice’, ‘Freedom’ and ‘Premium’. Each level of insurance cover can be purchased with whichever Participating Hospital group best suits your needs. Find your nearest hospital using our UK hospital finder.
