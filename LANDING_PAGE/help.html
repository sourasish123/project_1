Help Section

Welcome to the Help Center for our Hospital Finder Website! This guide will help you navigate our platform and make the most of our services.


1. Find a Hospital please follow this steps :
1) go to the home page
2) click on the search bar
3) type your location
4) find your nearest hospital
2. Identify a Disease please follow this steps :
1) go to the homepage
2) click the "Find a Disease"
3) give the picture input
4) give your query(symptoms)
5) know about your disease
3. Find a Doctor please follow this steps :
1) go to the home page
2) click on the "Find a Doctor"
3) type about your disease
4) get the doctor name for your disease
4. Book an Appointmen please follow this steps :
1) go to the home page
2) click on the "Book an Appointment" option
3) click on your preferred hospital where you want to book your appointment

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q1: Is the disease identification tool accurate?
=> Our system provides possible conditions based on symptoms entered. However, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a doctor for an accurate diagnosis.


Q2: How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?
=> Go to your Dashboard > Appointments > Select the appointment and choose "Cancel" or "Reschedule."


Q3: Do I need to create an account to use the website?
=> Some features, such as finding hospitals and doctors, can be used without an account. However, booking appointments and tracking medical history require an account.


Q4: How do I contact support?
=> If you need further assistance, you can reach out via our Contact Us page or email us at support@example.com.

We hope this guide helps you navigate our platform easily. Stay healthy and take care!
