Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device—such as your computer, smartphone, or tablet—when you visit a website. These files are used to identify your browser, store information such as login details, and remember preferences. They do not typically contain any personally identifiable information but can be linked to the personal data we store about you if applicable.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us offer a more personalized and user-friendly experience. We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences (like language and region), analyze how users interact with our content, and provide insights to improve our website performance. In some cases, cookies also help us deliver more relevant content and advertisements tailored to your interests.

Managing Cookies

You have the right to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to alert you when cookies are being used or to block all cookies. However, please note that disabling cookies may impact your user experience and some website functionalities may not work properly as a result.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use a variety of cookies on our website for different purposes. These include session cookies, which expire once you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your device until deleted or they expire. Functional cookies help enhance your browsing experience, while analytical cookies collect data to understand user behavior and optimize performance. We also use security cookies to help identify and prevent potential security risks.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may also incorporate third-party cookies from trusted partners like Google Analytics, social media platforms, or advertising networks. These cookies may collect data about your browsing habits across different websites and build a profile of your interests. While we do not control third-party cookies, we strive to work with providers that uphold data protection standards and offer clear opt-out mechanisms.

Performance and Analytics

Performance cookies collect aggregated data on how users navigate through our website—such as which pages are visited, how long users stay, and any errors encountered. These analytics help us understand what works well and what needs improvement, allowing us to deliver a better experience. We use this information strictly for internal analysis and improvements.

Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies are used to show you ads that are more relevant to you based on your browsing activity. These cookies may be placed on our site by advertising partners and are used to build a profile of your interests. They do not store direct personal information but identify your browser and internet device. You may see fewer targeted ads if you choose to disable these cookies.
